Our platform uses AI to support automatic subtitle generation and speech recognition. AI tools can help transcribe audio, generate subtitles, and enhance user experience. However, AI-generated subtitles may contain errors, especially when recognizing different accents, dialects, background noise, or informal speech.

To increase transparency, we notify users when subtitles are generated by AI. The platform clearly labels automatically generated captions and explains that the system relies on probabilistic language prediction rather than full comprehension of speech. Users can also report inaccurate subtitles or suggest corrections.

Since speech recognition systems may reflect social and cultural biases, the platform is designed to regularly test subtitle accuracy across different accents and speaking styles. User feedback will be incorporated into future improvements to reduce disparities in speech recognition performance.
